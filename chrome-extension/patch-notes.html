Dynamic Island V1.3

Here is what's new in the latest update!

New Settings Menu

Added a brand new settings menu accessible from the extension icon to easily toggle features and customize your experience.

Free Placement & Presets

You can now freely move the island anywhere on your screen (enabled by default) or change its preset positions to snap it to the edges!

Hide Island Option

Need more space? You can temporarily hide the island. It will seamlessly appear back when you refresh the page or open a new tab.

Advanced Lyrics Engine

We've added a multi-provider lyrics system to fetch lyrics faster, along with a sleek provider menu to swap sources on the fly.
